What is Itasha?

Itasha (痛車), literally "painful car", refers to vehicles decorated with anime, manga, or video game characters. This unique Japanese subculture combines automotive enthusiasm with otaku culture, creating mobile works of art.

The designs often feature vibrant colors, intricate illustrations, and sometimes even 3D elements that make the cars stand out in any setting.
